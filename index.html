01 / About

CJ H. Adisa.

Founder. Operator. Builder. Storyteller. Ten years in the room. Still showing up.

Founder & CEO C.H.A. LLC

Built across four divisions. Run from one operator's seat.

Operations and management professional with 10+ years of cross-sector leadership spanning disaster recovery, financial services, and multi-unit operations — now also the founder, builder, and operator of an 8+ product portfolio.

I founded C.H.A. LLC in 2019 and have spent the years since building what most people split across a team: the consulting practice, the SaaS portfolio, the content network, the publishing arm — and the systems that keep all of them moving without dropping the ball on any one of them.

My background isn't typical for a tech founder. I came up through bakery ownership, multi-unit restaurant management, personal banking, and federally regulated disaster case management. Every one of those roles taught me something I now use daily: how to build compliant systems, how to manage stress in high-stakes environments, how to lead people who are tired, and how to ship.

I work fully remote. I write the code, the copy, the books, and the strategy. The work is built to compound — quietly, repeatedly, on purpose.
